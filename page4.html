Mr. Ronnie Boy A. Mercado

Photo Journalism Representative- Paombong High School Inc. 

  1. For you, What is Photography? "Photography is about taking a picture with your heart because if you don't love what you do, then your picture will not be as good as you expect."
  2. Why do you love photography? "Because of photography, I realized what I am. Photography let me realized that a person's life can change in just one photo. Also because of Photography, I had friends that I know were real because we had the same passion and interest."
  3. What can you say to those who want to pursue photography? "My advice is that you believe in your own ability. As long as you capture from your heart, you'll have a good shot."
